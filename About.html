About Our Food Donation System

Every day, countless restaurants prepare more food than is consumed. At the same time, many communities and charities struggle to provide enough meals to those in need. Our Food Donation System bridges this gap by connecting restaurants with surplus food to charities that require it.

Our Mission

To reduce food wastage and fight hunger by creating a simple, reliable, and transparent platform where restaurants and charities can collaborate for social good.

How It Works

Restaurants list the food they have available along with the pickup time. Charities can then view the available donations and claim the ones that match their needs and location. The system ensures that food reaches the right place at the right time.
