Join Us

Together, we can make a difference. Whether you are a restaurant owner, a charity representative, or simply someone who cares about reducing food waste and fighting hunger, there’s a place for you in our mission.

For Restaurants

If you own or manage a restaurant, you can contribute by donating surplus food. Our system allows you to list food availability along with pickup times, so it can reach nearby charities that need it most.

For Charities

If you represent a charity or community organization, you can register to receive donations from local restaurants. You’ll be able to find food that matches your needs and location.

For Individuals

Even if you don’t run a restaurant or charity, you can still help. Spread the word, volunteer with local food drives, or connect us with organizations that could benefit from our system.

Join Us

Every small step counts. By working together, we can reduce waste, feed more people, and build stronger communities. Get involved today and be part of the change!
